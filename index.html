About ShopEasy

ShopEasy was inspired by the need to bridge the gap between farmers and consumers. We aim to provide an efficient and affordable platform where farmers can sell their products directly to customers. The idea originated from our team's desire to support local farmers and ensure customers receive fresh products at fair prices. This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
